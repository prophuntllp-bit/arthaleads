--- 2.1 LEAD CAPTURE & IMPORT ---

MULTI-SOURCE AUTO-CAPTURE:
Leads automatically flow into Arthaleads from multiple sources simultaneously:
- Facebook Lead Ads (Meta) — webhook-based, real-time, auto-token refresh
- WhatsApp enquiries — tagged with WhatsApp source label
- Google Ads — enterprise plan
- Website / WordPress contact forms — via plugin (8 form builders supported)
- QR Code forms — org-level and project-level
- CSV / Excel bulk import — with duplicate detection
- Manual entry — via Add Lead form
- Custom webhooks — for enterprise API integrations
Every lead is tagged with its source automatically.

QR CODE LEAD CAPTURE:
- Every organisation gets its own unique QR code
- Every project gets its own unique QR code
- Place QR on site hoardings, brochures, expo stalls, visiting cards
- Prospect scans QR → fills short form on their phone → lead lands in CRM instantly
- Source is automatically tagged
- Admins can download, print, or regenerate the QR code any time
- Available on Leads page and Projects page

FACEBOOK LEAD ADS INTEGRATION:
- Connect Meta/Facebook Ad account via OAuth
- Leads flow in automatically within seconds of form submission
- No manual export from Meta Leads Centre required
- Token auto-refresh so connection never breaks silently

WORDPRESS PLUGIN (v1.0.2):
- Captures leads from 8 WordPress form builders:
  MetForm, Contact Form 7, WPForms, Elementor Forms,
  Gravity Forms, Ninja Forms, Forminator, Fluent Forms
- All custom fields captured automatically

BULK IMPORT:
- Upload CSV or Excel file with hundreds of leads
- Paste markdown or CSV text directly (Paste-and-Import feature)
- Duplicate phone number detection — skips duplicates automatically
- Supports name, phone, email, project interest, source, notes fields

--- 2.2 LEAD MANAGEMENT ---

UNIFIED LEAD INBOX:
- All leads from all sources appear in one central list

- Filter by status, source, assigned agent, priority, date range

DUPLICATE PREVENTION:

LEAD EXPORT:

SECTION 5 — COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS
================================================================

Q: How do I add a new lead?
A: Go to the Leads page and click the "+ Add Lead" button at the top right.
You can also import leads in bulk from a CSV/Excel file using the Import
button, or connect Facebook so leads arrive automatically.

Q: How do I connect Facebook Lead Ads?
A: Go to the Automation page (Admin or Manager only). Choose Facebook Lead Ads,
connect your Meta account, and select your lead form. Every new submission
will then auto-import into the CRM.

Q: How does QR code lead capture work?
A: Every org and project has its own QR code. Open the QR icon from the Leads
page (for org QR) or the Projects page (for project QR). Download or print it.
When a prospect scans it, they fill a short form and the lead lands in your
CRM automatically with the source tagged.

Q: How do I generate an invoice?
A: Go to Bookings, open a booking, and click "Generate Invoice". Make sure your
org billing details (GST, PAN, logo, bank details) are filled in Settings first
so they appear on the invoice letterhead.

Q: How do I set up attendance?
A: Go to Settings → Attendance Settings to configure shift times and selfie
requirements. Agents then clock in/out from the Attendance page on their phone.

Q: How do I assign leads to my team?
A: Open any lead and use the Assign field. Or tick multiple leads and use the
bulk Assign action. You can also enable auto round-robin assignment in
Settings so new leads are distributed automatically.

Q: What happens to leads when an agent leaves?
A: Leads are never deleted when an agent is deactivated. All their leads remain
in the system and can be reassigned to other agents.

Q: Can I use Arthaleads on my phone?
A: Yes. Arthaleads is fully mobile-responsive and works on any browser on
iPhone or Android. There is also a PWA (Progressive Web App) you can add
to your home screen for a near-native app experience.

Q: Is there a limit on how many leads I can have?
A: No. There is no limit on the number of leads. Only the team member (seat)
count is capped by plan.

Q: How is data kept secure?
A: Each organisation's data is completely isolated (multi-tenant architecture).
Role-based access ensures agents only see their own leads. Every action is
logged in an audit trail. Data is never permanently deleted (soft delete).
All APIs are JWT-secured with HTTPS everywhere.

Q: Can I export my data if I cancel?
A: Yes. You can export all your leads as CSV or Excel at any time, even before
cancelling. Your data is always yours.

Q: How do I get support?
A: WhatsApp or call +91 80801 97945. Or email contact@arthaleads.com.
The in-app Help Bot can answer most questions instantly.
