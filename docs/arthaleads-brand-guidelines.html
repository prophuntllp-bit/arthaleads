Voice & Tone

Arthaleads speaks like a confident, experienced colleague in real estate — not a salesperson, not a tech company. Direct, warm, and India-aware.

✓ Arthaleads Voice

"Close more deals. Every lead captured. Every follow-up on time."

✗ Not Our Voice

"Leverage our AI-powered SaaS solution to optimise your lead conversion funnel."

✓ Arthaleads Voice

"Your team is working. Nothing is falling through the cracks."

✗ Not Our Voice

"Experience seamless workflow automation with intelligent lead assignment capabilities."

✓ Arthaleads Voice

"That Facebook lead from last night? Already assigned and followed up."

✗ Not Our Voice

"Real-time multi-channel lead ingestion with automated assignment protocols."

Writing Rules
Short sentences. One idea per sentence. Indian brokers are busy.
Active voice. "The system assigns leads" not "Leads are assigned by the system."
Real examples. Use "94 leads", "₹4.3 Cr pipeline", "15 agents" — not "many leads."
India context. Write "₹" not "$". Reference GST, RERA, Pune, Mumbai naturally.
No jargon. "Auto-assign leads" not "utilise intelligent routing algorithms."
Outcomes first. Lead with what the user achieves, not how the feature works.
